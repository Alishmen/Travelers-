About "Sintra Full Day"

Immerse yourself in the magical atmosphere of Sintra for a whole day! You will discover majestic palaces, picturesque parks, breathtaking views of the Atlantic, and learn about the history and legends of this unique region.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Included: Transfer, guided tour, palace tickets, lunch
  • Main highlights: Pena Palace, Quinta da Regaleira, Monserrate Palace, National Palace, Seteais Palace, Cabo da Roca, Azenhas do Mar, Praia Grande, Valley of Lakes
  • Price: $420
