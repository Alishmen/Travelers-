About "Fatima Tour"

The Journey of Faith Begins in Fátima
Your spiritual adventure begins in Fátima, one of the most venerated sanctuaries in the world. Discover the awe-inspiring Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary and the peaceful Chapel of the Apparitions, where the Virgin Mary is said to have appeared to three shepherd children—an event that continues to draw millions of devoted pilgrims each year.

Feel the deeply spiritual atmosphere as you join your guide for Mass, and take a moment to purchase a handcrafted rosary from a local artisan, a meaningful keepsake of your visit.

Continue your journey to Aljustrel, the quiet village where the story of Fátima began. Explore the modest homes and sacred landmarks connected to the visionaries, gaining insight into the humble origins of one of Christianity’s most cherished stories.

What to Expect
Duration: ~5 hours | Pick-up included from Lisbon, Sintra, or Cascais
• Discover one of Portugal’s most sacred destinations, deeply cherished by Christians around the world
• Attend Mass and explore key religious landmarks, including the Sanctuary of Fátima and the Chapel of the Apparitions
• Visit the tranquil village of Aljustrel, where the three shepherd children once lived
• Travel in comfort on a private tour—personalized and unrushed, for a truly meaningful spiritual experience

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Included: Transfer, guided tour, sanctuary visit, lunch
  • Main highlights: Fatima Sanctuary, Chapel of Apparitions, Basilica of the Holy Trinity, Aljustrel village
  • Price: $250

Tour Program

  1. Departure from Lisbon
  2. Excursion around the Fatima Sanctuary
  3. Visit to the Chapel of Apparitions and Basilica of the Holy Trinity
  4. Lunch at a local restaurant
  5. Visit to Aljustrel village — home of the three shepherd children
  6. Free time for prayer and souvenirs
  7. Return to Lisbon
