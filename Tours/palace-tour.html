About "Palace Tour"

Experience the Enchantment of Sintra Through the Eyes of Those Who Know It Best
Step into the storybook charm of Sintra with our passionate local guides—true insiders who know every hidden path, secret garden, and untold legend. More than just a tour, this is a deeply immersive journey into the soul of one of Portugal’s most captivating destinations.

Let us lead you beyond the obvious, as we unveil Sintra’s tucked-away treasures, majestic palaces, and breathtaking landscapes. Along the way, you’ll taste the region’s beloved sweets and hear the stories that bring its rich culture to life—from ancient myths to fascinating historical tales.

Our carefully curated stops include not only the iconic landmarks but also the lesser-known gems nestled in the surrounding countryside. Whether you’re wandering through lush, tranquil gardens or gazing in awe at centuries-old architecture, every moment is designed to stir your senses and deepen your connection to this magical place.

With each step, our guides will share their love for Sintra through engaging narratives, local curiosities, and authentic experiences that reveal the true heart of the village. This isn’t just sightseeing—it’s a journey into wonder, where history whispers from the stones and every view takes your breath away.

What to Expect
Get ready for a captivating sightseeing tour through the enchanting village of Sintra. Over the course of approximately 5 hours, you'll explore the area’s stunning landscapes and admire six magnificent palaces from the outside, each with its own unique history and charm.

To enhance your experience, you’ll also have the opportunity to choose one palace to visit from the inside, allowing for a deeper glimpse into the rich heritage and architectural splendor that makes Sintra so unforgettable.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Included: Transfer, guided tour, palace tickets, lunch
  • Main highlights: Pena Palace, National Palace, Monserrate Palace, Quinta da Regaleira
  • Price: $590

Tour Program

  1. Meet your guide and departure
  2. Excursion to Pena Palace
  3. Visit to National Palace
  4. Lunch at a traditional restaurant
  5. Walk through Monserrate Palace
  6. Finish the tour at Quinta da Regaleira
