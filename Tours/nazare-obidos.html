About "Nazare + Obidos"

Nazaré: Where the Ocean Meets Tradition
Our journey begins in the vibrant coastal town of Nazaré, renowned for its deep-rooted fishing heritage and the awe-inspiring waves of Praia do Norte—an international hotspot for surfers. Wander down the bustling Avenida da República, alive with the sights, sounds, and flavors of local life. Then, ascend to the clifftop district of Sítio, where sweeping views of the Atlantic Ocean provide a stunning backdrop for reflection and unforgettable photographs.

Óbidos: A Medieval Fairytale
We end the day in Óbidos, a picturesque medieval village that seems lifted from the pages of a storybook. Meander through its narrow cobbled streets, admire the whitewashed houses trimmed in bright colors, and walk along the ancient walls of its well-preserved castle. Be sure to try a glass of ginjinha served in a chocolate cup—a sweet tradition that perfectly captures the charm and warmth of this enchanting destination.

What to Expect
This is more than just a tour—it's a journey into the heart and soul of Portugal. From the raw, untamed energy of Nazaré’s waves to the storybook charm of medieval Óbidos, every stop is thoughtfully curated to leave a lasting impression. Expect a full-day adventure filled with culture, scenery, and unforgettable experiences. Convenient pick-up from Lisbon, Sintra, or Cascais is included, so you can relax and enjoy every moment.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Included: Transfer, guided tour, museum tickets, lunch
  • Main highlights: Nazare, Obidos fortress and old town, viewpoints, beaches
  • Price: $290

Tour Program
