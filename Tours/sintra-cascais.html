About "Sintra + Cascais"

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the captivating region of Sintra and Cascais with a knowledgeable local guide. Discover a perfect blend of iconic landmarks and hidden treasures as you delve into the rich history and breathtaking landscapes of these remarkable destinations.

Begin in the fairytale town of Sintra, where charming streets wind through a landscape steeped in magic. Stand in awe before the majestic Pena Palace, a colorful masterpiece perched high above the town. Continue your adventure at the mystical Quinta da Regaleira, where enchanting gardens, secret tunnels, and symbolic architecture invite exploration and wonder. Pause at the beloved Piriquita pastry shop to indulge in Sintra’s famous sweet treats, including travesseiros and queijadas.

From Sintra, journey to the dramatic coastline and stop at Cabo da Roca, the westernmost point of mainland Europe, where sweeping cliffs meet the vast Atlantic. Marvel at the natural beauty of Azenhas do Mar, a postcard-perfect village carved into the cliffs, and the powerful waves crashing at Boca do Inferno.

As you descend from the mountains, enjoy a scenic drive along the coast, where ocean views and lush landscapes provide the perfect backdrop.

Conclude your day in Cascais, a vibrant seaside town known for its elegant charm. Stroll through its lively streets, explore boutique shops, dine at exquisite restaurants, and unwind on its beautiful beaches—an idyllic end to a day full of discovery and delight.

What to Expect:
Experience the best of Sintra and its stunning surroundings on this immersive journey. Discover six breathtaking palaces nestled in the hills around Sintra, each offering its own unique charm and story. Along the way, soak in the beauty of the picturesque coastline, where dramatic cliffs meet the Atlantic Ocean.

Highlights include the charming cliffside village of Azenhas do Mar, the windswept Cabo da Roca—the westernmost point of continental Europe—and the dramatic seaside chasm of Boca do Inferno.

During your tour, you'll have the opportunity to visit up to two palaces from the inside, allowing for a deeper appreciation of their rich history, architectural splendor, and royal legacy. Whether you're drawn to the grandeur of Pena Palace or the mystery of Quinta da Regaleira, this journey offers a perfect blend of culture, nature, and discovery.

  • Main highlights: Pena Palace, Quinta da Regaleira, Cabo da Roca, Boca do Inferno, Cascais
